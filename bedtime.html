Once Upon a Time...

Once upon a time, there was a little girl named Sarah who loved to explore the world around her. Every night, she would go out into the woods and explore the trees, the animals, and the stars. She was always so curious and excited to learn more about the world around her.

One night, Sarah was out exploring the woods when she stumbled upon a magical tree. She was so excited to see it and she couldn't believe her eyes. She touched the tree and suddenly, the tree began to glow. She was so amazed and she knew that this was a special tree.

Sarah decided to take a closer look and she noticed that the tree was full of stars. She was so amazed and she couldn't believe her eyes. She touched the stars and suddenly, they began to twinkle and sparkle. She was so mesmerized by the beauty of the stars and she knew that this was a magical tree.

Sarah stayed with the tree for hours, just admiring the stars and the beauty of the night sky. She felt so peaceful and content and she knew that this was a special place. She thanked the tree for the wonderful experience and said goodnight.

The next morning, Sarah woke up feeling refreshed and energized. She knew that the magical tree had given her a special gift and she was so thankful. From that day forward, Sarah always remembered the magical tree and the beautiful night sky.

The End
